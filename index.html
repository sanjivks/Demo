Summary

This article provides a step-by-step guide on how to reset your password in Dynamics 365 CRM.

 

Prerequisites

  • Access to Dynamics 365 CRM
  • A valid user account

Steps to Reset Your Password:

  1. Navigate to the Sign-In Page:

    • Open your web browser and go to the Dynamics 365 CRM login page.

  2. Click on "Forgot Your Password?"

    • Below the login fields, you’ll see the link “Forgot your password?” Click it to begin the reset process.

  3. Enter Your Email Address:

    • Enter the email address associated with your Dynamics 365 account and click “Next.”

  4. Verify Your Identity:

    • You’ll be prompted to verify your identity through a security code sent via email or text message.

  5. Create a New Password:

    • Once your identity is verified, you’ll be prompted to create a new password. Ensure it meets the complexity requirements (e.g., 8+ characters, a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols).

  6. Confirm the Password Reset:

    • After entering and confirming your new password, click “Submit.” You can now log in using the new password.

Best Practices for Password Management:

  • Use a unique password for each account.
  • Regularly update your password.
  • Avoid using easily guessable information, such as birthdays or names.
